Classic Waffles

Description of Recipe

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tsp Salt to taste
  • 4 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tbsp White Sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 Cups Warm Milk
  • 1/3 Cup Melted Butter
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  4. Beat eggs in separate bowl.
  5. Stir in milk, butter, and vanilla to the eggs.
  6. Pour milk mixture into flour mixtures and beat until blended.
  7. Ladle better into preheated waffle iron.
  8. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
